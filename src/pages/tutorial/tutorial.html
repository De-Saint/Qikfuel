User Guide
Customer User Guide
  1. Click on Place Order
  2. Select Supplier based on State, Lga and Town / State and Lga / State and Town
  3. Select the product type and input your desire litres / pick from the list if its cooking gas
  4. A list of suppliers will be displayed based on search criteria such as Supplier's company name , product type and price
  5. You can search the list and select any supplier of your choice
  6. A preview of order details
    1. You can change the product quantity
    2. Pick Delivery Schedule
      1. Instant Delivery is delivered same day
      2. Schedule Delivery is delivered on the date selected
    3. Pick a delivery address
    4. Choose a payment plan
      1. Pay with POS
      2. Pay with Cash
  7. An order page is displayed showing your recent orders and their status
  8. The Supplier will call you before confirming an order so be careful with all orders placed
  9. Please cancel and delete any order NOT sure of or do not place unwanted order(s)
  10. You can track the order with the order number on the tracking page to ascertain the status of the product
  11. You can call the Transporter once its show "Pending Delivery
  12. You are expected to tap the CONFIRM ORDER button to confirm product has been delivered
  13. All Confirmed/Delivered orders are in your transactions Page
  14. Messages record all that happens on the app
  15. None means the person the transaction/order/request was tied to has deleted it

User Guide
  1. Once you have been activated by Qikfuel
  2. You can register transporters under you.
  3. All transporters registered under you must be activated by you before you can assign request to them
  4. You can also deactivate Transporter if need be
  5. All your Requests/Orders are shown on the Request Page
  6. You can Cancel an Order/Request by providing your reason(s) OR Accept an Order/Request by assigning a transporter to the order
  7. You can Call the Customer to confirm order before assigning a Transporter to the Request/Order
  8. All Confirmed/Delivered orders are in your transactions Page
  9. Keep track of your Supply limit and do well to inform the Admin once Supply Limit is reached
  10. Messages record all that happens on the app
  11. You are to add product(Petrol, Diesel, Kerosene and cooking gas) after registration and activation of account.
    1. Select product type
    2. Specify the price/litre that you are will to deliver
    3. Specify the quantity in litres that you want the app to capture
    4. Specify the minimum litres you can supply
  12. For Cooking gas input the amount any the kg(s) you are willing to deliver
  13. You can Delete and Add a new product
  14. You can Increase your total product quantity and also reset the prices for each of the product
  15. Account's Page will show your number of transactions and Amount'
  16. None means the person the transaction/order/request was tied to has deleted it
